Overview

About Microsoft Viva

Microsoft Viva is an integrated employee experience platform that gives users the ability to connect, gain insights, learn, and reach goals in the flow of your work.

Viva Goals is a goal management platform that leverages the OKR framework to align teams with organizational priorities, streamline collaboration toward a shared mission, and enhance business outcomes through continuous progress tracking.

What are the problems?

Key challenges we identified

01
Focused on a single goal framework
Viva Goals, designed around the OKR framework, falls short in meeting the needs of teams using diverse goal-setting methodologies.
02
Complex hierarchy
The OKR framework by nature has a complex hierarchy, which led to a steep learning curve for users to set up goals for their teams and organizations.
03
Too many terminologies
Terminologies such as Objectives, Key Results, Check-ins, and Alignment can make the product feel overly complex.
04
Needs an expert to implement
Due to the complex OKR methodology, implementing Viva Goals across teams often requires the guidance of an expert, such as an OKR champion.
05
Higher drop-off rate
With the help of data scientists we understood that the goal creation experience had one of the highest drop-off rates — closer to 47%.
Our Focus Group

Who are we building this for?

We focused on Senior Leaders and Leadership Teams (LTs) who are responsible for planning, creating, managing, and executing organizational goals. These users are most affected by the complexity of the current experience and have the highest drop-off rates.

Customer's Voice

Our customers' perspective on the current experience

We conducted user interviews to understand their friction and behaviour. We also accounted for existing feedback received from our customer base.

UX Audit: I conducted a UX audit on the existing experience to understand the gap to fill in order to satisfy user needs and business goals, and to find areas of inconsistent patterns and usability issues.

A whole new creation experience

Highlights
Hustle free goal planning made possible
  • Draft SystemBy introducing the draft option at the side nav, users can now plan goals for their teams. The draft space lets users refine and review their goals before publishing.
Highlights
Goal creation is no more a form
  • Goal ListWith Goal List in place, users no longer need a third-party tool to plan their team goals.
  • Inline Creation ExperienceGoal creation is made simple with the table view. The inline creation experience helps users store goal-related information effortlessly.
  • Auto-saveEditing and saving of goal information is simpler with auto-save, reducing multiple clicks.Data says: Most users perform planning in third-party tools like Notion, MS Excel, MS Word, ADO, etc. The new inline experience will make users feel right at home during their planning.
Highlights
Single stop solution for any goal framework
  • Highly FlexibleWith the ability to customize labels, users are no longer forced to follow a single goal framework.
  • Easy CollaborationUsers can invite collaborators to the goal list to get help in defining team goals.
Highlights
Highly customizable
  • Customize MetadataWith the add column functionality, users can choose the necessary metadata for their goals.From initial user testing, we realized users were not able to understand the purpose of list options, so we added visual cues and short descriptions for each — helping users realize when and how to use them.
Exploration

This is how the creation experience evolved

Through multiple rounds of iteration, customer interviews, and internal reviews, the experience evolved from a form-heavy approach to a fluid, table-first interaction model that feels native to Microsoft 365.

Highlights
Seamless interactive sub-goal creation
  • Simplified InteractionAfter evaluating initial designs with customers and partners, we arrived at a seamless sub-goal interaction — enabling users to enter sub-goal details without leaving to a different page or screen.
  • A win-win for Senior Managers and ManagersWith the sub-goal expand and collapse interaction in the table view, Senior Managers get a bird's-eye view of goals and sub-goals, while Managers can detail down the metadata of sub-goals without losing visibility of parent goals.
Highlights
Planning and execution of goals
  • Move Goal List to a TeamAfter planning and defining goals for a group, Senior Managers can now move the list to the specified team to make it functional — enabling both planning and execution within Viva Goals.
Highlights
Goal creation — Copilot experience
  • Upload your file, Copilot takes care of the restCustomer goals usually live in MS Word, MS Excel, or third-party apps. We integrated Copilot with Viva Goals so customers can simply import their file and Copilot formats their goals into a suitable table with necessary columns in place.
Highlights
Goal creation from templates
  • Template LibraryWe curated template sets to best reflect widely used goal frameworks. Templates also help users visualize their data in the format they expect.
Highlights
Managing and creating teams at ease
  • M365 Group IntegrationSenior managers and managers faced challenges creating teams and managing team settings. We introduced the ability to create teams directly from existing M365 groups — significantly reducing setup time and effort.
Highlights
Designing with Accessibility in mind
  • Inclusive by DesignAccessibility was an explicit consideration throughout the redesign — ensuring the experience works for all users regardless of ability or assistive technology needs.
